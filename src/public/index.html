Free Software Lab

Software development teams often need a test environment to consider investment in new software development trends. The Free Software Lab offers a technically equipped room and expert support to develop software prototypes with a variety of new technologies (LoRaWAN, Beacon, 5G, ...) and instruments (Speech Assistants, ...). Software development teams can learn about new software development processes and technologies in the Free Software Lab to prepare for R&D projects.
